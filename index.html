Unified Mobility Intelligence for Smart Cities

TransitLK is an integrated platform designed to solve fragmentation in Sri Lanka’s transportation ecosystem. We synchronize public transit, emergency response, and city infrastructure into one intelligent heartbeat.

The Problem

Unpredictable Bus Arrivals

Commuters waste over 3 hours daily waiting at stops due to a total lack of real-time tracking and unreliable scheduling.

Road Accidents & Unmonitored Drivers

High rates of preventable collisions occur because there is no automated system to monitor driver behavior or vehicle health.

Safety & Emergency Response Delays

Emergency services often arrive too late because crash notifications rely on manual reporting rather than instant data alerts.

Lack of Digital Passenger Engagement

Passengers are left in the dark with no info on bus capacity or safety, leading to a disconnected and stressful travel experience.

No Centralized Intelligence

City authorities lack the unified data needed to optimize traffic flow, resulting in permanent gridlock and high fuel waste.

The Solution

Real Time Public Transit

We end the 3 hour daily commute waste by providing passengers with sub minute accurate arrival times (ETAs) and vehicle capacity insights via a centralized framework.

Emergency Auto Response

Our Smart Bus Kits detect crashes or fires instantly. Verified location data is dispatched to Police and Fire departments via SMS protocols before manual reports even arrive.

Adaptive Traffic Control

Say goodbye to fixed timers. Our AI "sees" vehicle density and adjusts signal timing in real time to eliminate unnecessary idling and gridlock.

Pedestrian Guard

Smart ground level LED cues sync with traffic lights to provide visual safety signals for phone distracted pedestrians at high risk crossings.

Why TransitLK?

Sri Lanka's roads currently operate without real-time intelligence. This fragmentation leads to wasted productivity, preventable fatalities, and high fuel consumption.

TransitLK provides City Authorities, Police, and Transport Operators with the data they need to build a smarter, safer, and more resilient nation.
